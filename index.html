FAQ

Core Questions About FanBookings

Is FanBookings a ticket marketplace?

No. FanBookings is a fan-first booking knowledge platform that guides you to the safest platforms. We don't sell tickets directly - we help you find the right platforms to buy from.

What makes your recommendations trustworthy?

Every platform we recommend is verified with evidence tiers (A-C). We highlight fraud patterns, provide confidence labels, risk assessments, and last-reviewed dates. No affiliate bias - safety first.

How do you prevent showing scam sites?

We use evidence-based verification: official league/venue partnerships, reputable consumer sources, verified fan reports, and continuous fraud monitoring.

Which languages and countries do you cover?

Phase 1: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese for USA, Canada, UK, EU, Mexico, Japan. Phase 2 expands to more regions.

How current is the booking information?

Every guide includes a last-reviewed date. We maintain currency as platforms, policies, and fraud patterns change with regular updates.

Do you recommend based on affiliate commission?

Absolutely not. Recommendations are based purely on consumer safety, legitimate platform verification, transparency, and evidence-backed assessment.
