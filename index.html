Frequently Asked Questions

Is FanBookings a ticket marketplace?

No. FanBookings is a fan-first booking knowledge platform that guides you to the safest platforms. We don't sell tickets directly‚Äîwe help you find the right platforms to buy from.

How do you prevent scams?

Every platform we recommend is verified with evidence tiers. We highlight common fraud patterns, safe payment strategies, and provide confidence labels with risk levels for every recommendation.

What languages do you support?

We're building multilingual coverage including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and Arabic (RTL).

How current is your information?

Every guide includes a last-reviewed date. We're committed to keeping information current as platforms and policies change regularly.

Do you have a bias toward certain platforms?

No. We're not affiliate-driven. Our recommendations are based on consumer safety, legitimate verification, and transparency about trade-offs.

Which regions do you cover?

Phase 1 launches with USA, Canada, UK, EU, Mexico, and Japan. Phase 2 expands to South America, Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia.
